Flex size demo

Intro

This demo visually shows the contributions of flex-grow and flex-shrink to the size of a flex item.

The grey area is the initial size due to flex-basis. The green area is the additional size due to flex-grow. The blue area is the min-content size toward which flex-shrink reduces the size. Note that we can't visualise the for reference since we can't directly visualise the "subtracted size" due to flex-shrink unlike the additional size due to flex-grow.

All three boxes have their initial main size set to flex-basis: 20rem which is wider than their minimum size min-content (default).

Instructions:

  1. Resize the browser window to make the boxes in their initial size fill the whole width.
  2. Enable growing and / or shrinking.
  3. Resize the browser window to see the effect.
